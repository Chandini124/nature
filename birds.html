birds

Birds are fascinating creatures that add beauty and life to our environment. They are warm-blooded animals covered with feathers and are known for their ability to fly. Birds can be found in almost every part of the world, from forests and mountains to deserts and oceans.


Birds play an important role in maintaining ecological balance. They help in pollination, seed dispersal, and controlling insect populations. Some birds migrate long distances during different seasons in search of food and suitable weather conditions.

animals

Animals are one of the most important parts of nature and play a vital role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem. They live in different habitats such as forests, oceans, deserts, grasslands, and even in our homes. Animals can be classified into various groups, including mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fish.


Animals provide many benefits to humans. Domestic animals such as cows, goats, and chickens provide food and other useful products. Pets like dogs and cats offer companionship and help reduce stress. Wild animals contribute to biodiversity and help maintain ecological balance.

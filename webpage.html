Nature is the beautiful and essential part of our lives. It includes mountains, rivers, forests, oceans, plants, animals, and everything that exists naturally on Earth. Nature provides us with fresh air, clean water, food, and resources necessary for survival.


Spending time in nature helps reduce stress, improves mental health, and brings peace to our minds. The beauty of green trees, colorful flowers, flowing rivers, and singing birds creates a sense of happiness and relaxation.
